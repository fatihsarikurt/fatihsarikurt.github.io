I am a Principal Engineer in Thermal Hydraulics at Terra Power with over 13 years of experience in high-fidelity CFD applications, multiphysics, and multiscale modeling of nuclear reactor systems. I hold a Ph.D. and M.Sc. in Nuclear Engineering from Texas A&M University and a Bachelor of Science in Nuclear Engineering from Hacettepe University.

My expertise lies in developing and applying advanced computational methods to analyze and optimize the thermal-hydraulic performance of nuclear reactor systems. I have successfully led code modifications, conducted CFD analyses, and collaborated on various research projects.
